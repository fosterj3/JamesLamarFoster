Washington state Department of Education COVID-19 Communication

May 7, 2019
Photo by Ivan Aleksic on Unsplash

In this project, I made two timelines with the same data using two different timline packages - timevis and vistime. On this website I only show the vistime timeline due to compatability issues.

library(tidyverse)
library(timevis)
library(vistime)
#Create Dataframe of timeline

Date <-  as.Date(c("2020-02-26", "2020-02-28", "2020-03-03", "2020-03-06", "2020-03-13", "2020-03-17", "2020-03-18", "2020-03-20", "2020-03-23", "2020-03-23", "2020-04-06", "2020-04-07", "2020-04-08", "2020-04-15", "2020-04-21", "2020-04-27", "2020-03-03"))
